Young Guns

2023

A documentary about Young Guns, an art studio in Vancouver that works with high school students to help craft their creative work and philosophies. The video takes interviews from the studio director, staff, and past students to craft a holistic view of what Young Guns is, how it came to be, and its impact on the young art community. While I was the director, this project could not have been done without the help of Cynthia Gan (Producer) and Cole Slater (Director of Photography).
